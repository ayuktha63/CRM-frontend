Orque CRM

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

1. Who We Are

Orque CRM is a multi-tenant SaaS CRM product within the Orque platform. This Privacy Policy explains how personal and business data is processed when you use Orque CRM. For business CRM records stored by a customer organization, that organization typically acts as the data controller; Orque provides the platform as a processor/service provider.

2. Data We Process

Depending on how the Service is used, we may process:

  • Account identity — username, email, role, and organization membership (users are provisioned via the Orque platform and synced into CRM)
  • CRM business records — leads, contacts, accounts, deals, quotes, invoices, products, inventory, campaigns, activities, tasks, notes, tags, and attachments
  • Session and security metadata — login sessions, activity timestamps, and audit logs used for access control and accountability
  • Optional connected content — email and calendar data when a user connects Google Gmail or Google Calendar via OAuth
  • Notifications — in-app notification content delivered over a live stream

3. How We Use Data

We use the data above to:

  • Provide and operate Orque CRM features for your organization
  • Authenticate users (JWT access and refresh tokens) and enforce roles
  • Enforce product licenses and feature access
  • Support single sign-on (SSO) between Orque platform apps where enabled
  • Deliver notifications, maintain audit trails, and secure the Service

4. Storage and Isolation

CRM data is scoped by organization so one tenant cannot access another’s records under normal operation. Authentication tokens and related session information may be stored in your browser’s local storage so you remain signed in across page loads.

5. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared only as needed to operate the Service, for example:

  • User provisioning sync between the Orque platform (OPAC) and Orque CRM
  • Google APIs when you or your organization choose to connect Gmail or Calendar
  • Service providers or operators engaged to host and maintain the platform
  • When required by law or to protect the Service and its users

6. Retention

Data is retained while your organization’s tenant and accounts remain active on the platform. Sessions can be terminated by administrators. Deactivated users lose access; retention or deletion of organization business data is managed according to that organization’s policies and the platform operator’s operational practices.

7. Security Measures

Orque CRM uses JWT Bearer authentication, organization-scoped access controls, and role-based permissions. Password reset flows are designed not to reveal whether a given email address has an account. No security practice is perfect; please use strong credentials and report suspected issues to your administrator.

8. Your Rights

For access, correction, or deletion of account or business CRM data, contact your organization’s administrator. Individual end users typically request changes through their tenant admin, who manages users and records for that organization.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

Orque CRM relies primarily on browser local storage rather than marketing cookies. Items that may be stored locally include authentication tokens, access policy and license status for the session, and UI preferences such as sidebar collapse, dark mode, and last-selected dashboard.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact your organization’s administrator or the Orque platform operator that provides Orque CRM to your tenant.
