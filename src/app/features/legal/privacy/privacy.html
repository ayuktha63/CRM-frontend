Privacy Policy

Last updated July 20, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Orque (“Orque,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and deletes information when you use Orque CRM and related Orque platform services (the “Service”), including optional integrations with Meta products (such as WhatsApp Business Platform / Meta APIs) and Google services.

For business CRM records stored by a customer organization (tenant), that organization typically acts as the data controller. Orque provides the platform as a processor / service provider. This policy is Orque’s own Privacy Policy for the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account and profile information — username, name, email address, role, organization membership, and authentication credentials (users are provisioned via the Orque platform and synced into CRM).
  • CRM business data — leads, contacts, accounts, deals, quotes, invoices, products, inventory, campaigns, activities, tasks, notes, tags, attachments, and similar records you or your organization enter into the Service.
  • Automatically collected technical data — browser type, device and OS information, IP address, approximate location derived from IP, pages/features used, timestamps, referral URLs, and server/application logs needed to operate and secure the Service.
  • Session and security metadata — login sessions, activity timestamps, and audit logs used for access control and accountability.
  • Meta / WhatsApp Platform Data — when your organization connects Meta products (for example WhatsApp Business Platform), we may process phone numbers, business display names, WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) identifiers, message template content, message delivery/read status, conversation metadata, and other data made available through Meta’s APIs solely to provide messaging and related CRM features.
  • Google integration data — when a user connects Gmail or Google Calendar via OAuth, we may process mailbox and calendar content needed to provide those features.
  • Notifications — in-app notification content delivered over a live stream.

3. How We Use Information (Purposes)

We process information for these purposes:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve Orque CRM for your organization
  • Authenticate users (including JWT access/refresh tokens), enforce roles, and manage sessions
  • Enforce product licenses and feature access
  • Enable single sign-on (SSO) between Orque platform applications where configured
  • Deliver messaging and campaign features when Meta / WhatsApp (or other) integrations are enabled — including sending templates, tracking delivery status, and associating conversations with CRM contacts
  • Send service notifications and maintain audit trails
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to security incidents, abuse, and fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

We do not sell personal information. We do not use Meta Platform Data to build profiles for advertising unrelated to providing the Service your organization requested.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information only as needed to operate the Service:

  • Within your organization to authorized users under role-based access controls
  • Between Orque platform components (for example OPAC and CRM) for user provisioning and SSO
  • With Meta Platforms, Inc. and its services when your organization uses WhatsApp Business Platform or other Meta APIs (message send/receive and related API calls)
  • With Google when a user chooses to connect Gmail or Google Calendar
  • With hosting, infrastructure, and support providers under confidentiality obligations
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the Service and users

5. Meta Platform Data

If your organization uses Meta products through Orque CRM, “Platform Data” means data obtained from Meta (including WhatsApp Business Platform), such as identifiers, phone numbers, message metadata, and related content available via Meta APIs.

We use Platform Data only to provide and improve the messaging and CRM features your organization enables, in accordance with Meta’s Platform Terms and Developer Policies. We do not sell Platform Data. Access is limited to authorized personnel and systems that need it to operate the Service. When you disconnect an integration or request deletion, we delete or de-identify Platform Data as described below, unless law requires retention.

6. Storage, Security, and Retention

CRM data is scoped by organization so one tenant cannot access another’s records under normal operation. Authentication tokens and related session information may be stored in your browser’s local storage so you remain signed in. We use technical and organizational measures appropriate to the Service, including JWT Bearer authentication, organization-scoped access controls, and role-based permissions. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

We retain information while your organization’s tenant and accounts remain active, and for a reasonable period afterward as needed for backups, dispute resolution, security, and legal compliance. Session records may be terminated by administrators.

7. How to Request Deletion of Your Data

You may request deletion of your personal data, including Meta Platform Data associated with your use of the Service. To submit a deletion request, do one of the following:

  • Ask your organization’s Orque CRM administrator to deactivate your user account and request deletion of your personal data from the tenant; or
  • Email privacy@orque.io with the subject line “Data Deletion Request”. Include (1) your full name, (2) username or email used in Orque CRM, (3) organization / company name, and (4) a clear description of the data you want deleted.

We will acknowledge your request and complete deletion (or confirm what cannot be deleted due to legal retention requirements) within 30 days of verifying your identity and organization affiliation. Deletion may be performed manually or automatically. Business CRM records owned by your organization may require your administrator’s approval before removal.

If you delete your in-app account, or if Meta notifies us that you have removed our app’s access, we will delete associated Platform Data we hold, except where applicable law requires us to retain it.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Subject to applicable law and your organization’s policies, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data we hold about you by following the deletion and contact instructions in this policy. Individual end users typically work through their organization administrator for CRM business records controlled by the tenant.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

Orque CRM relies primarily on browser local storage rather than advertising cookies. Items that may be stored locally include authentication tokens, access policy and license status for the session, and UI preferences such as sidebar collapse, dark mode, and last-selected dashboard. You can clear these by signing out and clearing site data in your browser.

10. Children’s Privacy

The Service is intended for business use by organizations and their authorized adult users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction).

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change when we do. Continued use of the Service after an update means you acknowledge the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data access requests, or data deletion requests:

  • Email: privacy@orque.io
  • Or contact your organization’s Orque CRM administrator
